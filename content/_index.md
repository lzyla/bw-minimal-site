# Jan Kowalski

Researcher / Artist / Journalist


**Portrait:** /images/portrait.jpg  
*Portrait, installation context (2019)*


I work at the intersection of research, art, and data journalism. My focus is on how systems shape attention, memory, and public knowledge.

My practice combines essays, open source tools, and audio work that documents technology without spectacle. I value calm interfaces and thoughtful engagement with data.
